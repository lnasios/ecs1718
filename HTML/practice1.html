Sir Paul McCartney was an Animal Rights Activist, Singer, Filmmaker, Composer, Songwriter.

Paul McCartney is a former member of the Beatles and one of the most popular solo performers of all time.

During a decade full of political and social strife, the Beatles expressed the broader hopes of their contemporaries for peace, love and rock 'n' roll with a little rebellion sprinkled in.
